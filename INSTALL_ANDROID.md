### Step 1: Allow Unknown Sources

1. **Open the downloaded file** (from your notifications bar or file manager).
2. A security message will appear:
   > *"For your security, your phone is not allowed to install unknown apps from this source."*
3. Click on the **Settings** button.
4. Enable the **Allow from this source** option (or *Unknown sources*).
5. Go back using the navigation arrow; the installation should now begin.

1. After step 1, another window may open, titled **"App blocked by Play Protect"**.
2. **Do not click on "OK"**. Look for and click on **Details** or **More details** (often a small link at the bottom).
3. A new button will appear. Click on **Install anyway**.
